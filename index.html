Digital Forensics Investigation Exercise

Suspect's desktop. Their desktop could be accessed after discovering their password in the forensic disk image.

Conducted a full investigation in a simulated suspect scenario. Analyzed a forensic disk image using Autopsy and performed live analysis on a disk image to uncover evidence, reconstruct events, and create a report to judge if the suspect was innocent or guilty.

This project was ran similar to a capture-the-flag scenario, where tokens were found in important locations. For example, in files where suspicious information could be found, or on websites visited by the suspect relating to their "illegal" activities.

Tools: Autopsy, Ghidra, John the Ripper, steghide
